A hotel robot butler is equipped with a mobile robotic base and a secure storage box used for delivering items ordered by guests. Once an item is placed into the storage box, the robot is dispatched to deliver it to the designated room. It navigates autonomously throughout the hotel, including operating lifts, by relying on a built-in map and real-time sensor data. Upon arrival at the guest’s location, the robot allows access to the storage box through the entry of a personal PIN code, ensuring secure and contactless delivery of the requested items.

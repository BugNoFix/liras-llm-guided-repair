      <parameter name="lab_location">$input.lab_location</parameter>

        </condition>
      </action>
    </action>
  </agent1>

      <parameter name="barcode">$input.barcode</parameter>
      <parameter name="location">locked drawer</parameter>

        <condition name="Barcode Scanned">
          <parameter name="barcode">$input.barcode</parameter>
          <success>true</success>
        </condition>
      </action>
      <action name="Sort Samples">
        <parameter name="samples">$input.samples</parameter>

          <parameter name="samples">$input.samples</parameter>
          <success>true</success>
        </action>
      </action>
    </agent2>
  </agents>

  <action name="Log Sample Delivery">
    <condition name="Sample Delivered">
      <parameter name="barcode">$input.barcode</parameter>
      <parameter name="lab_location">$input.lab_location</parameter>
      <success>true</success>
    </condition>
  </action>

  <action name="Track Time between Sample Collection and Delivery">
    <parameter name="time">$input.time</parameter>
    <success>true</success>
  </action>

The first agent (`agent1`) has two actions: `Request Delivery to Laboratory` and `Log Sample Collection`. The `Request Delivery to Laboratory` action takes the `barcode`, `nurse_id`, and `lab_location` inputs and logs the sample collection. The second agent (`agent2`) has three actions: `Pick Sample from Locked Drawer`, `Scan Barcode`, and `Sort Samples`. The `Pick Sample from Locked Drawer` action takes the `barcode` input and picks the sample from the locked drawer. The `Scan Barcode` action scans the barcode of the picked sample and sorts it with the other samples. The `Sort Samples` action loads the sorted samples into the entry module of the analysis machines. The final action (`Log Sample Delivery`) logs the delivery of the sample to the laboratory, and the `Track Time between Sample Collection and Delivery` action tracks the time between sample collection and delivery.

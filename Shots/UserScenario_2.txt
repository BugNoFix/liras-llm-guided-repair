Three robots operate within a hospital environment: a mobile platform (Summit), a manipulator (PA10), and a mobile manipulator (Tiago) referred to as MP, M, and MM, respectively. The manipulator M is deployed in the hospital storage area and is responsible for loading requested items, such as towels, onto the mobile platform MP whenever these items are needed by nurses or doctors. Once loaded, MP autonomously navigates to the location of the requesting medical personnel. In cases where the item to be delivered is particularly heavy, such as large medical equipment, the mobile manipulator MM is tasked with reaching the nurse’s location to assist in unloading the equipment. When MP and MM are not engaged in delivery tasks, they continuously patrol specific areas of the hospital to prevent unauthorized access to restricted zones, such as radiotherapy rooms.
